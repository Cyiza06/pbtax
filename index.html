We Are P&B

our history

P&B Tax Services LLC was founded in 2019 with a simple mission: to help friends and family members file their income tax returns at a low cost while ensuring they received the maximum refund possible. As we witnessed the positive impact of our services on those closest to us, we realized the broader potential to assist a wider community of taxpayers. Driven by our commitment to providing affordable, high-quality tax services, we expanded our mission to help every taxpayer take full advantage of all available tax deductions and credits. Our goal is to maximize our clients' refunds and minimize their tax liabilities, ensuring they keep more of their hard-earned money. In addition to tax preparation, we recognized a growing need for professional representation in dealings with the IRS. We began offering IRS representation services to assist our clients with audits, disputes, and other tax-related issues, providing expert guidance and support. To further enhance our client services, we added comprehensive bookkeeping solutions to our offerings. By managing our clients' financial records with precision and care, we help them stay organized, compliant, and financially healthy, allowing them to focus on growing their businesses. Read more

our mission

Our mission at P&B Tax Services LLC is to deliver exceptional tax preparation, IRS representation, and bookkeeping services. We strive to ensure that every client benefits from every possible tax deduction and credit, securing the maximum refund or savings. Our dedication to accuracy, affordability, and personalized service sets us apart, as we aim to simplify the complexities of tax and financial management for our clients. We are committed to being a trusted partner in our clients' financial success, helping them navigate tax season with confidence and peace of mind.

Tax Preparation Services

1.Individual Tax Preparation :

At P&B Tax Services LLC, our comprehensive tax preparation services are designed to ensure that every client's tax return is accurate, optimized, and compliant with both federal and state regulations. Here’s how we handle each aspect of tax preparation: Filing Federal and State Returns


1.Thorough Assessment :

We begin by conducting a thorough assessment of your financial situation, reviewing income sources, expenses, investments, and any other relevant financial data.

2.Business Tax Preparation :

Services for different business structures (LLCs, S-Corps, C-Corps), tax planning, and quarterly filings.
Assistance for U.S. citizens living abroad, FBAR, and FATCA compliance.

3.Preparation of Tax Forms:

Our experienced tax professionals prepare all necessary tax forms, including:

  1. Form 1040 (Individual Tax Return): We accurately report your income, deductions, credits, and taxes owed or refunded.
  2. State Tax Returns: We prepare state tax returns specific to your state of residence, ensuring compliance with local tax laws and regulations.

Tax Planning and Optimization
  1. Year-Round Support: We provide year-round tax planning advice to help you make informed financial decisions that can minimize your tax liability in future years.
  2. Retirement Contributions: We advise on maximizing contributions to retirement accounts (e.g., Traditional IRA, Roth IRA, 401(k)) to potentially lower your taxable income.
  3. Investment Strategies: We offer guidance on tax-efficient investment strategies that can reduce your overall tax burden.

4.Electronic Filing

We facilitate electronic filing (e-filing) of your federal and state tax returns, ensuring a secure and efficient submission process.Maximizing Deductions

1. Comprehensive Review: We meticulously review your financial records to identify all eligible deductions. This includes:
  1. Standard Deductions vs. Itemized Deductions: We assess whether you would benefit more from taking the standard deduction or itemizing deductions based on your individual circumstances.
  2. Business Expenses:For self-employed individuals and small business owners, we ensure that all business expenses are properly documented and deducted.
2. Credits and Benefits: We explore all available tax credits and benefits that you qualify for, such as:
  1. Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC)
  2. Child Tax Credit
  3. Education Credits (e.g., American Opportunity Tax Credit, Lifetime Learning Credit).
